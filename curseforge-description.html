Flash Replay

Record anything. Then direct it like a film. Flash Replay is a client-side replay recorder and cinematic studio for Minecraft creators on NeoForge 1.21.1.

It is built for YouTubers, montage editors, server creators, builders and anyone who has ever thought: I wish I had recorded that.

Minecraft 1.21.1 - NeoForge 21.1.233+ - client-side - free download.

The short version

  • Capture: record gameplay into replay clips and Save Last moments.
  • Director camera: replay first, rebuild the camera move later.
  • Export: render real MP4 and GIF clips for editing or posting.

Set up recording in minutes

Flash Replay starts with clear client-side options, so new players can configure recording, Save Last, export and studio preferences before they need their first clip.

  • Open Flash Replay Options from the client UI.
  • Choose recording and Save Last behavior before playing.
  • Keep the workflow friendly for first clips while advanced tools stay available later.

Never miss the shot again

Flash Replay keeps a rolling replay buffer so your best moments do not disappear just because you forgot to start recording.

  • Always-on replay capture while you play.
  • Save Last for instant post-event clipping.
  • Highlight triggers for deaths, combat, elytra flight and danger moments.
  • Simple controls from the pause menu and options screen.

Shoot it like a film

Plain screen recordings lock you to the camera you had while playing. Flash Replay lets you capture the moment first and direct it after.

  • Camera rigs for dolly, push-in, crane, orbit, dolly zoom and constant-speed moves.
  • Subject framing modes for clean action shots.
  • Timeline keyframes with smooth interpolation curves.
  • Project, scene, shot and take organization for bigger edits.

Stage actors inside the replay

Turn replay entities into directable performers for thumbnails, machinima shots, fight scenes and cinematic storytelling.

  • Pose actors bone-by-bone.
  • Equip and group performers.
  • Save performance clips and replay them on cue.
  • Batch stills for character sheets and thumbnails.

Build the look in the tool

Flash Replay is not only about recording. It is about making the final shot look intentional.

  • Keyframe FOV, fog, time of day, weather and camera shake.
  • Use speed changes, slow motion and freeze moments.
  • Grade exposure, contrast, saturation, temperature and tint.
  • Frame with rule-of-thirds, safe frame and letterbox guides.

Export clips ready to post

Render your replay into the formats creators and editors actually use.

  • H.264, HEVC, ProRes and GIF workflows.
  • PNG sequences, batch stills and single-frame screenshots.
  • SSAA anti-aliasing and transparent-sky export options.
  • Audio bus controls for game, voice and scene audio.
  • Shader-friendly export workflows.

Simple first, deep when needed

Flash Replay is designed to be useful in the first two minutes and still powerful when the edit gets serious.

  • Simple: Save Last, quick replay, one-click export.
  • Director: camera rigs, shots, keyframes and Looks.
  • Pro: curves, actors, batch tools and full export settings.

Install

  1. Install NeoForge 21.1.233+ for Minecraft 1.21.1.
  2. Download Flash Replay from Modrinth or CurseForge.
  3. Drop the .jar into your mods folder.
  4. Launch the game.
  5. Open Flash Replay from the pause menu, or use Save Last when something worth saving happens.
